Welcome to CoinTrak

CoinTrak is the ultimate Cryptocurrency Companion app for iPhone. CoinTrak allows you to keep your portfolio in check with live price updates, conversion between any two currencies, a favoriting system, and even check into the balance of any BTC, ETH, or DOGE address on their respective blockchains. Keep your Cryptocurrency Portfolio on track with CoinTrak! CoinTrak has an expected App Store launch before the end of 2016.

Live Cryptocurrency Information

Cointrak currently employs the services of multiple data sources for its live, up to the minute price data. These sources include CoinMarketCap.com, CryptoCompare.com, Blockchain.info, Etherchain.org, and Dogechain.info.

Make use of the Main Ticker page for price info for the top currencies ranked by market cap. Tap any currency for price and market information, and double tap for historical data.

Live Cryptocurrency Conversion

Convert between any of the top 100 cryptocurrencies and fiat through the intuitive conversion page. Select which currencies you want to convert from and to from the selector wheels, enter a value, and convert!

Track Specific Coins

Simply swipe left and tap on any ticker on the main ticker page to add it to favorites, where you can keep a closer eye on it without all of the other coins in the way. The Favorites Page allows you to store whatever coins that you wish and saves them when you close the app, and you can swipe left and tap to remove any coin from favorites as easily as it was added. The interface is identical to the main ticker page, and you can double tap to bring up the chart info just like in the main page.

Track your Investment

Check the balance of any Bitcoin, Ethereum, or Dogecoin address and store them to keep track of transactions to and from yours, or anyone else's publicly available address with the easy to use Wallet Page! Simply enter the desired address in the corresponding field, press save, and you can check the balance and USD value of anyone's BTC, ETH, or DOGE address.
